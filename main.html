Veer-Zaara (2004) is a timeless Bollywood romantic drama directed by Yash Chopra, focusing on love, sacrifice, and India-Pakistan relations. It follows Indian Air Force pilot Veer Pratap Singh, who falls in love with a Pakistani woman, Zaara Hayaat Khan.
  • Shah Rukh Khan as Veer Pratap Singh
  • Preity Zinta as Zaara Hayaat Khan
  • Rani Mukerji as Saamiya Siddiqui
  • Supporting cast: Amitabh Bachchan, Hema Malini, Manoj Bajpayee, Divya Dutta
